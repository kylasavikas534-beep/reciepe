MAGGI

INGREDIENTS

  • maggi noodles-1packet
  • maggi tastemaker-1sachet
  • water-1/2cup
  • oil(optional)
  • vegtables(onion,carrot,peas)

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Boil water Pour 1½ cups of water into a pan and place it on the stove. Bring it to a boil.
  2. Add noodles Break the Maggi noodle cake into 2–4 pieces and add it to the boiling water.
  3. Add tastemaker Sprinkle the tastemaker evenly into the pan.
  4. Cook Let it cook for about 2 minutes, stirring occasionally so the noodles don’t stick
  5. Check consistency For soupy Maggi: leave a little water. For dry Maggi: cook a bit longer until water reduces.
  6. Serve hot Turn off the stove and enjoy your Maggi!
